Welcome to PermitFlow

Your streamlined path from concept to operation. PermitFlow connects you with FlowBot — your AI-powered guide — to navigate every step of the permitting process.

Permit to Design 🖊️

  • Define your project scope and objectives.
  • Gather required zoning, environmental, and safety guidelines.
  • Submit preliminary plans for review.
  • FlowBot can help identify missing documents and flag potential compliance issues early.

Permit to Build 🏗️

  • Finalize architectural and engineering drawings.
  • Secure contractor licenses and insurance documentation.
  • Schedule inspections and milestone approvals.
  • FlowBot can track submission status and remind you of upcoming deadlines.

Permit to Operate 🏢

  • Complete final inspections and safety certifications.
  • File operational compliance forms with local authorities.
  • Receive your occupancy or operational permit.
  • FlowBot can generate a closing checklist and archive your permit history.
💡 Tip: Ask FlowBot “What’s my next step?” or “Show me my checklist for [phase].”
